Availability of exim

Exim is available from a number of FTP sites. It may also be supplied on some GNU CDs or with other software distributions.

Please note that this mirror list has suffered from bitrot recently. I am about to go through and remove dead mirrors, updates others etc, but this takes a bit of time to do properly. My apologies for the list being currently inaccurate. It may be better to look at this mirrors list
